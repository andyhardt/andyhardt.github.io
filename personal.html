Personal

Aside from mathematics, I moonlight as a swimming commentator. I've commentated the MIAC Conference Championships in 2018, 2019, 2020 and 2022, the Men's Big 10 Championships in 2018, the Men's NCAA Division I Championships in 2018, and the Central Zone Division I Speedo Sectionals in 2019.

I've done a small amount of writing for SwimSwam.com, much of which can be found here.

I also enjoy competing in triathlons, and was a member of the University of Minnesota Triathlon Team.
