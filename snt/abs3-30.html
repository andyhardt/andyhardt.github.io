Student Number Theory Seminar 2019-20

3/30/20: Emily Tibor -- Introduction to Klyachko Models for GL_n

Abstract: Whittaker models for representations of GL_n have been featured in a number of student number theory seminar talks this year. In this talk, I will introduce a generalization of this model, called Klyachko models, which are mixed Whittaker-symplectic models, and discuss their properties. I will begin by recalling Whittaker models and their significance, which will lead into a more general discussion of why one might be interested in the study of unique models. Then, I will introduce Klyachko models for GL_n and explore questions about their existence and uniqueness over finite and non-Archimedean local fields.
